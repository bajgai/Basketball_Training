# Basketball Training — Curry-Based Shooting Program

A structured 1-hour basketball shooting drill program based on Stephen Curry's workout routines, adapted for an intermediate player working on three-point energy transfer.

## Source Material

Built from analysis of 4 video sources:
1. Stephen Curry's Basketball Workout During The Off-Season (with Brandon Payne)
2. MasterClass: Stephen Curry (Full Course)
3. Curry MasterClass Part 1
4. Curry MasterClass Part 2

## Quick Start

**Go straight to the routine:** [1-Hour Shooting Routine](routines/1-hour-shooting-routine.md)

## Player Profile

- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Strength:** Solid mid-range shot
- **Working on:** Three-point energy transfer — getting power from legs through the shot at distance
- **Court:** Half court
- **Equipment:** Ball and hoop
